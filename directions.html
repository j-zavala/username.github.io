Driving Directions

Select a new start and end point to see the directions render on the map and inside the directions panel to the right of the map to get to or from UAlbany events. Can double click on map to zoom. You can also use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out of the map. If you pick up the little yellow dude from the bottom right corner and drop him somewhere on the map, he will then show the street view of the area of where you dropped him. In street view you can see how the buildings look and whats around by clicking your mouse and dragging it to move the view around.
